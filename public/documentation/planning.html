Problem Set 3: Planning Your Web Application
      This is project plan for building a community based web application, intern.fht.psu.ac.th , whose goal is to provide information to students of Prince of Songkla University about different internship sites best to work at and provide platform for interactive relationship between sites and students.
 

Usage Scenarios
User classes
  • Student, who registers for internship
  • Hotel, Resort and Restaurant (Site), who accepts student for internship
  • Administrator, who maintains the system
  • Visitors, can be lecturers, who come to observe about the current situation or can be another students, who come to see the best site to work at by checking its rating and reviews of previous students

Exercise 2
Offline Alternatives
      The best offline alternative is the Career fair. Career fairs happen annually or bi- annually. It provide different opportunity to students to get face-to-face interaction with different representative of sites. Generally, career fairs are good, but expensive to organize. The system would provide best and cheap alternative to such events. It provide all the information online so have broader range of users. Online medium of communication are fast, cheap and very effective.
 
Online alternatives
      We are building the system for Prince of Songkla University. They have a system to assign and manage interns in different sites. We were to able to study the project. We are trying to provide an extension to that system.
 
We found :
  • http://www.globalplacement.com/
  • http://www.placement-uk.com/pages/index.php
      Both focus on global perspective which does not include within our scope of study. They were profit-oriented system. We are focusing on Hotel and Tourism sectors. Additionally, they are not community based products. There are many more. However, they don’t include rating, reviews and interactions between students and sites which we are planning to implement.
 
      We can get suggestion from these websites regarding students and sites information which might help us in deriving forms, evaluation and developing a bridge between sites and students.
 

Exercise 3
Online alternatives
So far, none of the online alternatives fall on criteria we are planning to develop.
  • The system currently been used at PSU manages and assign sites to interns. We are able to study the system. It does not include the information. Sometimes the internship records of student are not recorded. Also, when student wants to know information regarding their assigned internship sites, they have to personally contact previous students. If we could provide platform to share their experiences, it would be easy to get the information.
  • Internship sites can register themselves, and can provide information about their internship programs to students.The system provide platform for communication between sites and students.
  • Registration process is the crucial part of the system. It provide the system valuable information about students and internship sites. Everyone have to registers except anonymous observer/visitor.
  • As the system going to use online medium (i.e. emails), everyone who registers must have usable email account. Valid email account is must. Though the system will include all the valuable information along with view restriction, email are used for confirmation and authentication of inputted information.
